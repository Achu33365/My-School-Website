Campus Life





Primrose, is a one of a kind pre school initiative of Thakur Group; a group that has been offering quality education from K.G to P.G level for the last two decades, and today has 8 campuses with 22 institutes, over 50000 students and 4000 plus employees. Malcolm Gladwell has said that, “If you can hold the attention of children, you can educate them”. At Primrose, the child is at the center of everything we do. Primrose Pre School is designed to help children develop their cognitive, psychometric and communication skills. It does not stop here; we also work on our students' emotional and social development. All this is achieved through the school environment and infrastructure which have been developed considering the needs and comfort of a toddler.
